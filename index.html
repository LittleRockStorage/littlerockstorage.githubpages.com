FAQ

What is Littlerock Storage?

We offer easy affordable vehicle storage in Littlerock California. Storage is uncovered, and fully fenced on a loose surface lot.

How Many Vehicles Can I Store?

You can store up to 10 vehicles per person.

Can I store oversized vehicles?

Yes, you can store semi trucks, large construction equipment and aircraft.

Is a deposit required?

No. We do not require deposits. Once you complete your online booking request you will be charged the storage fee for the first month to reserve your storage spot.
